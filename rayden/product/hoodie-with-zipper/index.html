About The Project

Reliable and Efficient for Cruise Ships.

We've been working with HarborSupply Ship Chandlers for the past five years to stock our cruise liners, and their service has been impeccable. Handling the supply chain for a large cruise ship with hundreds of guests on board is no easy task, but HarborSupply makes it seamless. From fuel and spare parts to guest provisions and medical supplies, they’ve covered everything we need. The deliveries are always on time, and their team works efficiently to get the items loaded without causing any delays. They also provide excellent customer service and keep us updated on any potential supply issues well in advance. I highly recommend them to any large-scale operatio need, whether it's food supplies for the crew or critical engine parts. What stands out is their flexibility—they’re available 24/7, even when we’ve had last-minute requests. The quality of their provisions is consistently fresh, and they’ve built strong relationships with local ports, ensuring swift delivery. Their ability to source rare parts for our older vessels is a lifesaver, especially in emergency situations. Highly recommend them for any large-scale cargo operation.
